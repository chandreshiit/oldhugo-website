AI Lab

The AI Lab is dedicated to building state-of-the-art models, tools, and algorithms for next generation technologies. The lab is speaheaded by Chandresh Kumar Maurya. Our main work revolves around using AI, ML, and NLP techniques to solve problems in text, vision, and speech domain. Look at our publications and project pages to know more about our work. We are always on the hunt of talented individuals interested in giving a break in the AI, ML, and NLP field. Shoot an email to chandresh at iiti.ac.in with brief details of your research interest and updated CV.
